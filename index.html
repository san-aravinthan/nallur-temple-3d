MODEL OF NALLUR KANDASWAMY DEVASTHANAM

Jaffna, Sri Lanka — a Dravida temple to Lord Murugan (Kandaswamy). The red clock-gopuram and great golden porch face the sandy forecourt in the east; the saffron Shanmuga and Gubera Raja Gopurams rise to the south and north; within: the Vel sanctum, a 48-pillar mandapam, the holy pond and Poonthottam.
